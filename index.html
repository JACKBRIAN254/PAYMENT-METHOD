Payment Methods

At AGALLO'S, we offer a variety of secure and convenient payment methods to make your shopping experience smooth and easy. Below you will find the available payment options and details on how they work.

Available Payment Methods

Credit and Debit Cards

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Simply enter your card details at checkout, and your payment will be processed securely.

PayPal

For your convenience, we also accept PayPal. If you have a PayPal account, you can log in and complete your purchase using your PayPal balance or linked bank accounts.

Bank Transfer

We also support direct bank transfers for larger purchases. If you prefer to pay via bank transfer, please select this option at checkout, and our team will provide the necessary bank details for the transfer.

Mobile Payments (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay)

You can use mobile payment options like Apple Pay, Google Pay, and others to complete your purchase quickly and securely from your smartphone or tablet.

Security and Privacy

Your security is our top priority. We use the latest encryption and security protocols to ensure that all your payment details are safe and secure. Our website is fully PCI-DSS compliant, meaning it meets all industry standards for processing payments securely.

All payment transactions are processed using secure SSL technology to protect your data from unauthorized access. We never store sensitive payment information on our servers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I know if my payment was successful?

After completing your payment, you will receive an email confirmation with the details of your order. You can also view your order status in your account under "Order History."

2. Can I change my payment method after placing an order?

Unfortunately, once an order is placed and payment has been processed, we cannot change the payment method. Please ensure your payment details are correct before confirming your order.

3. Is it safe to enter my payment information on your website?

Yes! We use SSL encryption and comply with the highest standards of security to protect your payment details. Your information is safe with us.

4. Do you accept international payments?

Yes, we accept international payments, but please note that currency conversion fees may apply depending on your payment method or card issuer.
